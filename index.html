Generative AI in Dream Manipulation:
The Future of Sleep Science

A community website for those who seek material at a higher academic level.


  Our Aim

  • To inform and to support
    •
There has been a huge demographic facing issues with sleeping, hence we would like to set up an interactive platform for both those who want to share their experiences and authorities. Having difficulties in sleeping seems to be a problem for themselves only, yet lacking support from others. Hence the website serves as a companion, staying beside you every night when sleeping seems to be a luxury.

On the way of seeking an antidote to insomnia, they may want a one-stop service. Serving as an authoritative platform for the dissemination of groundbreaking research, insights, and discussions on the application of generative AI in the field of sleep science. This site seeks to bridge the gap between complex scientific understanding and public knowledge, allowing victims to share their thoughts and support each other in the long way.
